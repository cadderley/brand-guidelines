Usage Rules

Accent Orange — Restricted

Reserved for operator control retention statements and coordination advantage callouts only. Never use for section headers, decorative highlights, or general emphasis.

Primary Blue — Authority Anchor

The Networks8 node is always Primary Blue in governance diagrams. CTA backgrounds use Primary Blue exclusively.

Deep Navy — Hero and Dark Sections

Use for full-bleed backgrounds, cover panels, and CTA bands. White or Secondary Blue text only on Deep Navy surfaces.

No Decorative Gradients

Avoid gradients unless reinforcing structural hierarchy. Canvas is the base page surface — not white.

Writing Rules
01
Sentence case everywhere. Headlines, labels, diagram nodes. Title case for formal proper nouns only.
02
Headline register. Write like operator documentation — not campaign copy. Describe execution scenarios, not marketing promises.
03
Body paragraphs under 40 words. Infrastructure executives read in dense environments. Lead with consequence, not context.
04
No transformation language. Avoid: transform, unlock, disrupt, end-to-end, synergies, value-add.
Diagram Language

≥
Ecosystem is always plural. Label the bottom "Delivery Ecosystem" — not a single vendor or team.
Approved Diagram Types
  • 01
    Execution Gap Diagram
    Divergence between capital approval and field reality. Thought leadership, problem framing, conference presentations.
  • 02
    Three-Model Comparison
    Internal PMO | Networks8 | Turnkey EPC. Networks8 always center, elevated. Homepage and sales decks.
  • 03
    Vendor Ecosystem Map
    Grid format: engineering, construction, suppliers, ROW, permitting, inspection, logistics, finance. Never radial.
  • 04
    Deployment Lifecycle Overlay
    Networks8 spans all execution phases from funding through multi-market expansion.
  • 05
    Operator Control Retention
    Two columns: what operators retain / what Networks8 provides. Comparison sections and sales collateral.
